Why Donate Blood?

Blood donation helps save lives and is a simple yet invaluable gift to those in need. One donation can save up to three lives. Regular blood donations ensure that hospitals always have blood available during emergencies.

Eligibility to Donate Blood:

  • Age between 18 and 65 years
  • Weight at least 50kg
  • Good general health
